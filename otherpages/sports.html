Sports games are video games that simulate real-world sports, allowing players to engage in virtual renditions of activities like football, basketball, soccer, and more. They often strive for realism, replicating the rules, strategies, and dynamics of the actual sports. These games can range from highly realistic simulations with accurate player likenesses and physics to more arcade-style experiences with exaggerated action. Multiplayer modes are common, enabling players to compete against friends or others online, adding a social and competitive dimension to the gaming experience.
